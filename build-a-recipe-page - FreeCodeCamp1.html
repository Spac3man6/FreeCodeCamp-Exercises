The Perfect Medium Rare Steak

This recipe not only involves sourcing the right ingredients. It requires a special use of mathematical calculations to get the perfect fire and distance from it to the grill.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak ( width of your choice)
  • Sea Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter/Tallow
  • Rosemary ( If possible)

Instructions

  1. Make a fire comprised mostly of charcoal and some wood. Wait 20-30 minutes or untill fire is ready.
  2. Heat cast iron pan while seasoning the steak. If possible, prep the steak 1 hour before.
  3. Sear steak edges for 2 minutes then place 4 minutes on each side and remove cast iron from the direct fire after this.
  4. Finish cooking the steak 5 minutes on each side while receiving indirect heat. During the last few minutes place the butter/tallow in the cast iron and baste your steak.
  5. Allow steak to rest for 10 minutes while not slightly elevated, with no sides touching a surface.
  6. Cut against the grain. Enjoy!
